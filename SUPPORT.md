# Support

For installation help, rendering bugs, or feature requests, search the
[existing issues](https://github.com/TomasOrtega/TeX-for-Gmail/issues) and open
a new issue if needed.

For a bug, include:

- the extension version or commit;
- Firefox or Chrome and its version;
- the shortest formula that reproduces the problem;
- the steps taken and the result you expected; and
- relevant extension-console errors with private Gmail data removed.

Do not post private email content, credentials, or suspected vulnerability
details. Report security and privacy vulnerabilities through
[GitHub private vulnerability reporting](SECURITY.md).
